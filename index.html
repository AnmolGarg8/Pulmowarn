THE DEVICE

A dual-sensor wearable patch.
Worn on the throat overnight.

Designed for comfort and clinical accuracy. The PulmoCare patch is low-profile, skin-safe, and capable of 24-hour continuous monitoring on a single charge.

  • Dimensions: 7cm × 4cm × 1cm
  • Weight: under 35g
  • Connectivity: BLE 5.0
  • Battery: 24h continuous
