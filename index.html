THE SOLUTION

One device.
Two sensing modalities.

PulmoCare combines acoustic sputum detection with transcutaneous CO₂ sensing — monitoring both the cause and the consequence simultaneously.

🎤

MEMS Acoustic Sensor

50–1000 Hz range, −42 dBV/Pa sensitivity. Detects rhonchi, wheezing, and sputum-related breath sounds in real time.

🫁

tcPCO₂ Sensor

Continuous transcutaneous blood CO₂ monitoring. Tracks trends, not just snapshots — catching the rise before it becomes dangerous.

🧠

On-Device ML Inference

ARM Cortex-M4 runs a lightweight classifier at <8mW. No cloud dependency. Real-time sputum risk scoring at the edge.

📱

3-Layer Alert Cascade

Layer 0: Patient notification (sputum). Layer 1: Caregiver push alert (CO₂ rising). Layer 2: Emergency SMS (critical CO₂).
